1. What's a prime lens?

2. What's APS-C cameras? What is its full form?

An APS-C (Advanced Photo System - Classic) camera refers to a camera that uses an APS-C sized image sensor, which is smaller than a [full-frame sensor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Advanced_Photo_System#/media/File:APS_Film_Frame_Sizes.svg) but larger than sensors found in most smartphones and compact cameras.
